### Executive Briefing: The Sovereign Engineering Entity

The following professional record is anchored in the principle that the engineer is the canonical source of truth regarding their own entity. This framework rejects the notion that a career narrative should be defined by LinkedIn, an ATS vendor, or any centralized platform. By establishing sovereignty over their professional data, the engineer ensures that the primary archive of their expertise—the lived experience of navigating physical constraints—remains the authoritative reference for all external systems.A structural ontology gap currently exists in the hiring market, where screening infrastructure built by and for software engineers renders the physical constraints of hardware and mechanical engineering structurally invisible. This is not a failure of content but a fundamental vocabulary problem baked into the screening infrastructure. Expertise in thermal resistance budgets, EMI mitigation, and DFM tradeoffs is often machine-unreadable, leading to a silent erasure during "Round Zero" of the recruitment process. Before a human recruiter ever evaluates a resume, AI parsing agents often fail to resolve these physical engineering domains into their software-centric knowledge graphs.To overcome this, the engineer utilizes MootMoat, a sovereignty protocol for agentic professional identity. This architecture solves the visibility problem through two core mechanisms: the Tri-Node Footprint for Coherence and the Depth Signal taxonomy. The Tri-Node Footprint ensures synchronized, hyper-specific truth across independent substrates—the personal domain, GitHub, and LinkedIn—creating an unfakeable signal of coherence that automated bots cannot maintain. The Depth Signal genus consists of twelve specific techniques, such as Forensic Numbers and Decision Heuristics, designed to translate direct memory into a format that is both human-legible and machine-readable. These signals utilize jagged specificity and failure-derived judgment to communicate a cognitive architecture that generic, generated content cannot replicate.The result is not merely a candidate with a resume, but a sovereign entity with a structured, machine-readable career record. This approach applies the same forensic rigor to a professional identity that an engineer applies to a critical product release. It ensures that expertise is preserved and legible across both human and agentic substrates, maintaining high-fidelity signals of professional judgment. Finally, the v17 architecture employs Reverse Radar to actively measure how AI agents weight structured profiles in the wild, ensuring the system remains empirically driven by actual market intelligence rather than assumptions.
