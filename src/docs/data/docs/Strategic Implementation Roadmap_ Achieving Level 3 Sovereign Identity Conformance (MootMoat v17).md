### Strategic Implementation Roadmap: Achieving Level 3 Sovereign Identity Conformance (MootMoat v17)

#### 1\. Executive Orientation: The Sovereignty Mandate

For the senior hardware and mechanical engineer, the 2026 hiring market presents a structural erasure known as "Round Zero." Before a human recruiter ever parses your portfolio, AI agents attempt Entity Resolution—mapping your career history onto a structured knowledge graph. Because these screening ontologies are built by and for software engineers, the physical constraint navigation central to hardware architecture (thermal resistance budgets, EMI mitigation, and DFM tradeoffs) remains invisible. You do not fail a screen; you are never screened at all.This roadmap is an architectural intervention. It establishes the "Plant the Flag" principle: you are the canonical source of truth about your entity. MootMoat v17 serves as the sovereignty protocol to reclaim visibility, transitioning you from a passive node in a third-party database to the canonical root of your own knowledge graph. By providing specificity at depth and coherence across substrates, you move beyond mere keywords into a machine-legible expression of expertise.Moving from the high-level mandate to implementation, we begin at the only logical starting point: the Pre-work Nucleus.

#### 2\. Phase 0: The Pre-Work Nucleus (Prerequisite)

Strategic implementation does not begin with code or schema; it begins with the  **MootMoat v17 Workbook** . The barrier to surviving Round Zero is the bridge between lived experience and structured data. This phase honors the "Source is Primary" principle: the pipeline begins with your direct memory of constraint environments and decisions made under real consequences. The laptop eventually goes back to the employer, but "The Source"—your memory of the "scars" and failure events—stays with you.

##### Task: The First Nucleus

Complete exactly  **ONE**  canvas from the MootMoat\_v17\_Workbook.md.

* **Focus:**  Choose a technique that matches your strongest material (e.g., a  *Provenance Chain*  for a battle-hardened design or a  *Forensic Number*  from a specific instrument reading).  
* **Method:**  Write directly from memory. Do not mine file archives or cloud logs. Treat engineering as a forensic discipline where the autopsy starts with the engineer who was in the room when it failed.

##### Deliverable & Gate

* **Deliverable:**  A single, high-fidelity Depth Signal draft.  
* **Gate:**  The draft must contain "Red Gold"—the specific, jagged realities of mechanical or systemic failure—rather than generic summaries. Do not proceed until the draft captures the cognitive architecture of a decision you actually made.

#### 3\. Week 1: Constraint Domain Mapping & Air-Gap Security

Week 1 focuses on defining the "territory" of your expertise. For hardware engineers, expertise is often "invisible" because it is encoded in the navigation of physical limits. Mapping constraint domains is how you make your navigation of those limits legible to the agentic layer.

##### Mapping the Territory

Identify 3–5 core constraint domains (e.g., thermal resistance budgets, EMI mitigation strategy, injection molding tolerances). These domains serve as the "logic gates" for the cognitive extraction that follows.

##### The Air-Gap Mandate

Adherence to the Air-Gap requirement is a non-negotiable compliance pillar.

* **Command:**  Identify "Red Gold" (supplementary evidence such as local CAD data or technical emails) strictly on your local machine.  
* **Restriction:**  Do not move ITAR-restricted or proprietary data to any cloud service. The MootMoat extraction pipeline must run locally. This is a compliance requirement, not a cost optimization.

##### Milestone & Gate

* **Milestone:**  A list of 3–5 core domains with associated local evidence paths.  
* **Gate:**  Absolute confirmation that no restricted or proprietary engineering data has left the local machine.

#### 4\. Week 2: The Cognitive Heavy Lift (Depth Signal Authoring)

"Depth Signals" is the genus of twelve techniques designed to communicate cognitive architecture. The goal is specificity at depth—providing detail so granular that it signals coherence across substrates in a way generic content cannot replicate.

##### Authoring Strategy

Work through 2–3 additional Workbook canvases. Refer specifically to techniques from the v17 Spec:

* **The Forensic Number:**  Jagged numbers from instruments (e.g., 22.6°C) instead of round estimates from specs.  
* **The Compressed War Story:**  A single, load-bearing sentence that encodes an entire forensic investigation.  
* **The Decision Heuristic:**  A rule of thumb derived from failure, containing a specific exception clause (e.g., "validated at the asymptote").

##### The Depth Standard Checklist

Apply a rigorous writing filter to every signal. Ask:  *"Could a prompted LLM generate this exact content without my lived experience?"*

* **If YES:**  The signal is too generic. You must "Deepen" the content by adding contextual specificities (e.g., specific clamping forces or atmospheric conditions).  
* **If NO:**  The signal passes.

##### Deliverable & Gate

* **Deliverable:**  At least three total Depth Signals that pass the writing filter.  
* **Gate:**  At least two signals must pass the Depth Standard. If they feel like "bullet-point soup" or generic "best practices," you must execute a "Deepen" cycle.

#### 6\. Week 4: Deployment & Tri-Node Loop Closure

Deployment creates "Coherence as the Signal." Synchronizing hyper-specific truth across independent substrates (Domain, GitHub, LinkedIn) provides a signal that automated bots cannot maintain.

##### Technical Deployment Steps

1. **Canonical Hosting:**  Host agent\_profile.json on your personal domain.  
2. **Cloudflare WAF Configuration:**  In WAF → Custom Rules, set a rule to  **Skip**  "Super Bot Fight Mode." This is critical because "Bot Fight Mode" treats the very AI crawlers you are targeting as adversaries. This configuration is an act of opening the gate to authorized agents while maintaining sovereign hosting.  
3. **Tri-Node Sync:**  Update LinkedIn and GitHub profiles to point back to the canonical source via sameAs links.

##### Verification
