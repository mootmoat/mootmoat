### MootMoat v17 Implementation Study Guide: The Senior Hardware Engineer’s FAQ

#### Core Frequently Asked Questions

##### 1\. What is Round Zero and why should I care about it?

Round Zero represents the hidden pre-screening phase where AI parsing agents evaluate your professional profile before any human recruiter sees it. These agents perform Entity Resolution, attempting to map your experience onto a structured knowledge graph to determine if you are a coherent "entity" for a specific role. If the machine cannot resolve your expertise into its internal ontology, you are silently discarded. You do not fail a technical screen; you are simply never screened at all.

##### 2\. My resume is strong. Why am I not getting calls?

The 2026 hiring market suffers from a structural "ontology gap" because most Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) were built by and for software engineers. These systems have no native vocabulary for physical constraint navigation, such as thermal resistance budgets, EMI mitigation, or DFM tradeoffs. Consequently, your genuine hardware expertise remains invisible to the automated gatekeepers. MootMoat fixes this by translating your career record into a machine-readable format that the screening infrastructure can actually process.

##### 3\. What is The Source and why is it primary over archived files?

The Source is the primary archive of lived experience—your direct memory of constraint environments, failure events, and decisions made under real consequences. It predates every CAD file, log entry, and engineering change order (ECO) you have ever produced. In the MootMoat v17 framework, the engineer’s brain is the canonical repository, not an old hard drive. Because you are writing from memory, the "scars are the receipt," allowing you to document expertise without requiring corporate subpoenas or breaching security protocols.

##### 4\. What is the air-gap requirement and when does it apply to me?

The air-gap requirement mandates that the extraction pipeline must run locally on your machine to prevent sensitive data from reaching the cloud. It applies to you whenever you are dealing with ITAR-restricted, proprietary, or export-controlled engineering data. This is a compliance non-negotiable, not a cost-saving measure. By keeping the extraction process offline, you ensure that proprietary IP is never uploaded to a third-party LLM or SaaS provider.

##### 5\. What is a Depth Signal, and how many techniques are there?

A Depth Signal is a specific technique used to translate "The Source" into formats that are simultaneously machine-readable and human-memorable. There are exactly twelve techniques in the v17 taxonomy, including the Forensic Number, Compressed War Story, and Decision Heuristic. Each technique is designed to communicate cognitive architecture through jagged specificity that generic summaries cannot replicate. These signals act as an enrichment layer, making your expertise legible to AI agents while remaining authentic to human reviewers.

##### 6\. What is a Structural Rhyme and how is it different from the other eleven Depth Signal techniques?

The Structural Rhyme is the flagship technique within the Depth Signal genus because it serves as the primary schema mechanism for machine legibility. It maps a physical constraint domain isomorphically onto a digital or computational analogue, using a shared failure mode as the proof. While other techniques provide narrative or numerical depth, the Structural Rhyme feeds directly into the isomorphicProof block of the JSON-LD schema. It is the core architectural tool for making ATS-invisible expertise accessible to knowledge graphs.

##### 7\. Could I just prompt an LLM to generate my Depth Signals for me?

No, because according to the Provenance Principle, an LLM can formalize your experience, but it cannot discover it. You must write from lived experience first, as an LLM cannot generate the specific, jagged details of a failure it has not lived through. The architect provides the raw material from "The Source," and the LLM’s role is strictly limited to enforcing schema compliance and structural formatting. Specificity at depth is the only way to communicate real cognitive architecture.

##### 8\. What is the Tri-Node Footprint and why is Coherence the actual signal?

The Tri-Node Footprint is the practice of maintaining synchronized, hyper-specific career data across three independent substrates: your personal domain, GitHub, and LinkedIn. The actual signal being sent is Coherence, rather than cryptographic proof. Automated bots and low-fidelity candidates cannot maintain multi-substrate consistency across complex domains like thermal resistance or DFM tradeoffs. Synchronizing these "jagged" signals across multiple surfaces provides an unfakeable indicator of a human operator working under real-world constraints.

##### 9\. How does v17 handle ITAR-restricted program details?

The current v17 protocol does not yet solve the problem of authenticated private endpoints for cleared reviewers; this is a named architectural gap. To maintain compliance, the protocol relies on "The Source," where you write from memory rather than transcribing protected files. This approach ensures that you can describe the nature of your expertise and the constraints you navigated without breaching an air-gap or exposing restricted data. No subpoena is required because the data remains an expression of your professional judgment, not a corporate asset.

##### 10\. What is Level 1 conformance and why does it come before anything else?

Level 1 conformance focuses on ensuring you are "Entity Resolvable," which is the minimum requirement to prevent being discarded during Round Zero. It establishes your presence on at least three nodes with valid JSON-LD context and accessible metadata files. This is considered "table stakes"—the functional floor below which your expertise is effectively invisible to AI screening agents. Differentiation only becomes possible once you have passed this initial visibility threshold.

##### 11\. What is the honest case against doing this?

Implementing MootMoat requires a significant upfront time investment in "cognitive extraction" that many engineers may find taxing. Furthermore, the relative weighting that AI agents assign to structured schema data versus traditional keyword density remains an open empirical question. While the protocol is designed to surface expertise, Level 3 conformance is ultimately a bet on the future of the hiring market. If the underlying expertise is not present, no amount of schema work can manufacture an edge.

##### 12\. Where do I start right now, today?

The first concrete step is to open the MootMoat\_v17\_Workbook.md and complete a single canvas. You should pick the Depth Signal technique that matches your strongest material, such as a specific failure event or a hard-won design rule. This completed canvas serves as the "nucleus" for your entire deployment. Everything else—including the JSON-LD encoding, the LinkedIn synchronization, and the website deployment—is downstream of this initial extraction.
